Type 25 Gunsmith Page

Good fast-firing weapon for close range and fair at mid range. It has fast close-range time to kill with reasonable range. Not recommended for long or very long range due to the recoil. Try to tap/single or 2-3 rounds burst fire at long range. Recoil pattern is hard to remember but is quite constant across shots. Medium recoil pattern deviation constant across shots except when near the recoil pattern edges where recoil pattern deviation becomes higher. Medium aim shake with no attachments.

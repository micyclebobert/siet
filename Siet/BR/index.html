Mods:

Currently, there are 9 types mods in BR. Mods can't be equipped on Anhialator, War Machine, Tempest, Launchers, or Melees.


Extended Mods: Increases ammo capacity.

Level 1 (White/Common): Increases ammo capacity by a 5 bullets

Level 2 (Green/Uncommon): Increases ammo capacity by a 5 bullets

Level 3 (Blue/Rare): Increases ammo capacity by a 10 bullets

Level 4 (Purple/Epic): Increases ammo capacity by a 10 bullets

Level 5 (Void Mags/Orange/Legendary): Increases ammo capacity by 20 bullets and decreases reload time but when reloading bullets remaining in the mags will be destroyed. Can not be equipped on Shotguns.
 

Dense Fire Mods: Decreases aim spread. On shotguns, it acts as a choke (less pellet spread).

Level 1 (White/Common): Decreases aim spread by a few % or Decreases Shotgun spread by a few %

Level 2 (Green/Uncommon): Decreases aim spread by a few % or Decreases Shotgun spread by a few %

Level 3 (Blue/Rare): Decreases aim spread by a few % or Decreases Shotgun spread by a few %

Level 4 (Purple/Epic): Decreases aim spread by a few % or Decreases Shotgun spread by a few %
 

Stabilizer Mods: Decreases recoil.

Level 1 (White/Common): Decreases recoil by a few %

Level 2 (Green/Uncommon): Decreases recoil by a few %

Level 3 (Blue/Rare): Decreases recoil by a few %

Level 4 (Purple/Epic): Decreases recoil by a few %

Level 5 (/Orange/Legendary): Decreases recoil by a few %
 

Longshot Mods: Increases damage range.

Level 1 (White/Common): Increases damage range by a few %

Level 2 (Green/Uncommon): Increases damage range by a few %

Level 3 (Blue/Rare): Increases damage range by a few %

Level 4 (Purple/Epic): Increases damage range by a few %
 

QRF Mods: Increases hip-fire accuracy.

Level 5 (/Orange/Legendary): Increases hip-fire accuracy by a few %
 

Fast Trigger Mods: Increases fire of rate.

Level 5 (/Orange/Legendary): Increases fire of rate of M21 EBR and XPR (only equipable to M21 EBR and XPR)
 

Flashlight Mods: Blinds enemy for 

Level 5 (/Orange/Legendary): Increases rate of fire by a few %
 

Muffled Mods: Decreases aim spread. On shotguns, it acts as a choke (less pellet spread).

Level 5 (/Orange/Legendary): Increases rate of fire by a few %
 

Runner Mods: Increases sprinting speed after aiming for a few seconds.

Level 5 (/Orange/Legendary): Increases sprinting speed after aiming by a few %

Classes:

There are twelve classes that the player can choose between at the start of the game. Each member of a team can choose only one unique class i.e. nobody in the same team can have a same class.

  • Scout - Sensor Dart and Tracker
  • Clown - Toy Bomb and Anti-zombie
  • Medic - Medical Station and Medic
  • Ninja - Grapple Hook and Dead Silence
  • Defender - Transform Shield and Reinforced
  • Mechanic - EMP Drone and Engineer
  • Airborne - Ejection Device and Lightweight
  • Trickster - Psychosis and Alert
  • Trap Master - Electric Trip Wire and Territorial Effect
  • Poltergeist - Active Camo and Voidwalker
  • Smoke bomber - Cluster Smoke Grenade and Smoke Perspective
  • Hacker - Ice Pick and Hard Wired

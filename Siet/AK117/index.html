AK117 Gunsmith Page

Good medium-firing weapon for mid and long range. Low recoil allows the weapon to be easily used in long range. Recommended for flanking from far away. It has good hip fire accuracy and first (especially during the last few bullets) but easy to control recoil deviation. Easy to remember recoil pattern and medium aim shake.The is a high kick during the first few bullets but then it gradually stops. Medium fire rate makes recoil controlling even easier.
